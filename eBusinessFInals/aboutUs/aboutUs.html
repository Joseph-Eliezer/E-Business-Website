About Us

At PureWave, we are dedicated to revolutionizing residential water quality with our innovative, electric-free filtration systems. Our mission is to ensure every household has access to clean, safe drinking water, enhancing health and well-being. Driven by sustainability and cutting-edge technology, we strive to provide reliable and efficient water solutions. Trust PureWave for pure, healthy water directly from your tap.

PureWave Water System Inc. is a forward-thinking start-up committed to making clean water accessible to everyone, regardless of their location. Our innovative approach focuses on electric-free water filtration systems, ensuring environmental sustainability and ease of use.

Our flagship product, the PureWave Filtration System, embodies our mission to bridge the gap in access to clean water across diverse regions. Founded by a passionate group of students—Orbie Assi A. Versoza, Joseph Eliezer R. Viray, Cherry Rose A. Sarroza, Veverly G. Villaluz, Jholo D. Tallado, and Vincent Gabriel D. Valera—in May 2024, our company is dedicated to solving one of the most pressing issues of our time.

Headquartered in Balanga, Bataan, PureWave Water System Inc. aims to deliver safe, pure drinking water directly to households without the need for electricity. Join us in our journey to ensure that clean water is a fundamental right for all.

Our Mission

At PureWave, our mission is to transform the way households access and consume water. We are committed to providing innovative, electric-free filtration systems that deliver clean and safe drinking water. By prioritizing sustainability and advanced technology, we aim to enhance the health and well-being of families while minimizing environmental impact. Our goal is to empower communities with reliable, efficient water solutions that contribute to a healthier future.

Our Vision

Our vision at PureWave is to lead the evolution towards sustainable water management in residential settings. We envision a world where every home has effortless access to pure, contaminant-free water. Through continuous innovation and a deep commitment to quality, we strive to set new standards in water filtration technology. We aspire to be a trusted partner in promoting health, sustainability, and environmental stewardship globally.

Our Core Values

Innovation: Constantly pushing the boundaries of technology to deliver state-of-the-art filtration systems.
Sustainability: Minimizing environmental impact through energy-efficient, electric-free solutions.
Quality: Ensuring the highest standards of reliability, durability, and safety in every product.
Customer Focus: Listening to and meeting the needs of our customers with exceptional service and support.
Integrity: Conducting business with transparency, honesty, and respect for our customers and the environment.
